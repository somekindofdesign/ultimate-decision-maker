History of the Game

Created in 1998 by Sam Kass and Karen Bryla, Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock is based off the traditional Chinese game Rochambeau.

It's more commonly known in Western countries as Rock Paper Scissors and not only is it a bit of fun, it has been used as an objective decision making tool for a very long time.

The popular sitcom 'The Big Bang Theory' helped to popularise Kass and Bryla's modern version.
